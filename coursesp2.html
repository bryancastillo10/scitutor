Interesting Trivia and Facts

During summer, the Eiffel tower is about 15 cm taller

In the season of summer, heat on the surrounding rises resulting to volume expansion. This phenomenon when particles of a substance is heated up and allows them to move more which takes up a larger volume is called thermal expansion. In contrast, during winter by which temperature drops, it causes the particles to contract again. Usually, it is observed on large structures such as the Eiffel tower,bridges which has expansion joints that allows them to adjust on expansion and contraction without taking any damage to the structure.

The air bag from the cars is generally composed of salt sodium azide, a very toxic substance

As collision occurs, the car's sensor is triggered with an electrical impulse by which dramatically raises the the temperature of the salt. This salt is then decomposed into nitrogen gas which is a harmless gas, allowing for the air bag to expand.
