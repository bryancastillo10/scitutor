About Us

This tutorial website is free and provides fundamental learnings to students in the field of sciences such as biology, chemistry, and physics. In addition, we plan to add more features by adding other scientific fields as we collect lots of lectures and questions. A website that offers a lot of questions or problems commonly encountered by students during their studies on sciences. You can have access to tons of questions and basic lectures to simply understand concepts in a specific scientific field. Through this, students are encouraged to learn more with the aid of our online services. If you don't see your questions you're looking for, you can ask on us through our email. For further questions or donations for the improvement of the website, you can e-mail us here.

The author of the website was a biological engineering and chemistry graduate from Mapúa University, Philippines. He obtained those degree last August 2018 and immediately took the Chemistry Licensure examination on October 2018 to become a registered chemist. Today, he is now a laboratory chemist and pollution control officer in a private company that provides services in environmental analysis. With the curiosity to develop new skills, he self studied basic website development and computer programming. As a result, this website was developed in order to assist students pursuing the same profession.

What We Do?

We provide online courses on science subjects at college level. Courses available in this website were initially chemistry subjects. The five main areas of chemistry which are inorganic chemistry, analytical chemistry, physical chemistry, organic chemistry, and biochemistry. Other subjects are to be developed especially in the fields of biology and physics. The field of biology will focus on molecular biology and biotechnology and then physics is focused on fundamental concepts in physics for college level.
